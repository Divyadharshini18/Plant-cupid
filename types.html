Plant Types and Care Tips

Cupid Peperomia (Peperomia scandens)

The Cupid Peperomia features heart-shaped leaves and is known for being low-maintenance.

Care Tips:

  • Watering: Every 10 days; allow soil to dry out.
  • Light: Bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Soil: Well-draining potting mix.

Fern

Ferns are lush, green plants that thrive in humid environments.

Care Tips:

  • Watering: Keep the soil consistently moist.
  • Light: Indirect sunlight or partial shade.
  • Humidity: High humidity is preferred; mist regularly.

Cactus

Cacti are drought-resistant plants that thrive in dry conditions.

Care Tips:

  • Watering: Every few weeks; allow soil to dry completely.
  • Light: Full sun exposure is ideal.
  • Soil: Use cactus mix or well-draining soil.

Flowering Plants

These plants produce beautiful blooms and attract pollinators.

Care Tips:

  • Watering: Regularly during blooming season; avoid waterlogging.
  • Light: Bright light to encourage flowering.
  • Fertilizing: Use a balanced fertilizer during the growing season.
